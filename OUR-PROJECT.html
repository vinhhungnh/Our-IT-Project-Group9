J.A.R.V.I.S LITE

Topic

In this project, we plan to develop and build a mobile app using AI technology to help users manage their everyday tasks. In particular, users can utilize this task management tool to convert complex tasks into a series of smaller and simpler activities. In addition, users can modify their task lists, such as sorting, prioritization of activities and schedule, so that they can fulfill each work with flexibility. Furthermore, an automatic measuring and surveillance system is provided for work progress. Users will therefore be able to view their job progress on a frequent basis via charts and statistics. In the end, users can record and share all accomplished tasks in the archive as achievements with others.

The outcome we expect in this project is that users can improve their working style and enhance productivity when dealing with large workloads. In particular, users can split hard tasks into simpler ones to implement more easily. Besides, we also hope to make our app convenient to users with the proposed useful features. Thus, users can handle their tasks efficiently and optimally.

Motivation

The presence of advanced science is gradually improving life quality. However, this means the amount of work that people have to do is increasing. When faced with a large volume of complex tasks, many people feel stressed and confused. To address the aforementioned issue, developing a task management application is an appropriate solution to assist people in working more efficiently. This is the driving force behind our decision to develop this application. However, the significance and interest in this project do not end there. In this project, we want our application to be appealing to both novices and experts. As a result, in addition to useful features, this application includes a user-friendly interface. Furthermore, we always prioritize the development of applications that are in line with technological trends. In today's IT world, the primary goal of applications is to assist users in optimizing their work through management or automation solutions. In fact, providing a task management application provides users with convenience and flexibility in the working process while reducing resource waste. Overall, our project will demonstrate three prerequisites to users: assisting users in getting organized, enhancing their styles, and increasing their concentration.

Landscape

There are various types of task management tools on the market. While many apps have similar features, the level of detail varies depending on whether users want to use the tool for personal or professional reasons. When using a tool for personal needs, users can simply request simple features to keep everything up to date, such as Evernote, Google Tasks, and Microsoft Todo. They are useful for focusing on a particular task but are not the best tool for working, in general, when users have a lot of clients, priorities as well as deadlines. Things become more complicated when it comes to professional work. Users will almost certainly want more powerful features to track their progress, report on where their effort is being spent, and update team collaboration statuses. Some professional task management software also enables users to set automatic reminders or to compare estimated time to actual time for task completion. For example, there are some popular task management apps for teams such as Todoist, task, and Microsoft Planner. In this project, we wanted to find a balance of simplicity and efficiency in our product. To fulfill that desire, my team will provide a product with a simple and user-friendly interface that makes it easily accessible to newbies. Besides, it also ensures useful features for users who work in professional environments.
